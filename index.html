Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our private chef services and booking process.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for intimate dinners and 4-8 weeks for larger events. However, we do accommodate last-minute requests when possible, so feel free to reach out regardless of your timeline.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Chef Julian specializes in creating bespoke menus that accommodate all dietary needs including vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, keto, and allergies. Every menu is customized to your specific requirements.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the greater metropolitan area and surrounding regions. For destination events or travel outside our standard service area, additional travel fees may apply. Contact us to discuss your location.

Do you provide serving staff?

Yes, our Exclusive and Ultimate packages include dedicated service staff. For the Popular package, Chef Julian handles all preparation and service personally. Additional staff can be arranged for any package upon request.

What is included in the service?

All packages include menu consultation, ingredient sourcing, on-site preparation, cooking, plating, and cleanup. We bring all necessary equipment and leave your kitchen spotless. Wine pairing recommendations are also available.
